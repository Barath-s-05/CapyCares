5-Minute Breathing Exercise

5:00

How to do this exercise:

  1. Find a comfortable seated or lying position
  2. Close your eyes and take a deep breath in through your nose for 4 seconds
  3. Hold your breath for 4 seconds
  4. Exhale slowly through your mouth for 6 seconds
  5. Repeat this cycle for the duration of the exercise
  6. Focus on your breath and try to clear your mind
